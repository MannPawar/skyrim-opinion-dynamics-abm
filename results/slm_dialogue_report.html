Generative NPC Dialogue from an Opinion-Dynamics ABM

Each line below is generated by a small language model conditioned on an NPC’s emergent political opinion from the agent-based simulation.

How this works. Three layers. A stochastic agent-based model evolves a political opinion p for each of 1,010 named Skyrim NPCs over a Civil War campaign (p → 0 Imperial, p → 1 Stormcloak). A per-NPC lore knowledge base (scraped from the UESP wiki) supplies each character’s identity and a sample of their own canonical lines to anchor voice. The opinion and lore, not a hand-written script, are passed to a local small language model (llama3.2:3b via Ollama), which voices the character in-context. The ABM is the source of truth; the lore KB grounds identity; the SLM realizes the dialogue.
